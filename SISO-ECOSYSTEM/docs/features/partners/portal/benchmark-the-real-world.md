Guest facilitator: **Bernstein ALLi burn Garrison**

## Why benchmark it?
TRW combines a Discord-style community with high-frequency training, mentor access, and an internal marketplace. Understanding what works (and what fails) helps us evolve the SISO partner workspace while keeping our hashtag channels intact.

## 2. Where TRW Falls Short (and how we avoid it)
- **Security breaches** – a 2024 incident exposed 968k users, emails, and chat logs. citeturn1search0  
  → We add security banners, 2FA, session management, and routine transparency posts in `#announcements`.
- **Personality cult** – content leans on Andrew Tate’s persona; platform risk if a single voice falters. citeturn1search6  
  → Spotlight multiple SISO mentors and partner success stories to spread credibility.
- **“Escape the matrix” positioning** – compelling marketing but can erode trust for corporate clients. citeturn1search2  
  → Frame SISO workspace around measurable partner value: client wins, commission growth, quality enablement.

## 3. How We Apply the Playbook Without Renaming Channels
1. **Channel structure stays the same** – we keep `#welcome`, `#commissions`, `#app-plan-generator`, etc. Enhancements sit on top (panels, missions, mentor banners) instead of renaming them to “campus”.
2. **Daily mission cadence** – scheduler posts daily embeds (mirroring TRW’s 8 a.m. drops) while streak counters live in `enablement/engagement`.
3. **Mentor visibility** – `shared/contracts` defines `MentorProfile`; workspace header shows “Next live call” with join link; replays stored in `enablement`.
4. **Marketplace panel** – `growth-hub` exposes internal gigs, surfacing referrals or plan follow-ups without inventing new channel names.
5. **Security trust layer** – use TRW breaches as cautionary example; highlight our audits and controls.

## 4. Suggested Roadmap (6 sprints)
1. Extend channel registry with grouping metadata (no renames).
2. Build shared panel templates + retrofit current panels.
3. Implement mission scheduler + streak tracking.
4. Ship mentor directory + live session banners.
5. Launch marketplace MVP tied to revenue ledger.
6. Deliver security transparency widget + auth hardening.

## 5. Success Metrics
- Daily mission completion ≥ 60% of active partners.
- Mentor touchpoints ≥ 2 per partner per month.
- Time-to-first earning (deal/payout) reduced by 20%.
- Security NPS question ≥ 4.5/5 after transparency roll-out.
- Marketplace gigs closed/month doubled over baseline.
